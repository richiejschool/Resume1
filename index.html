New York City's Migrants

Rich Mendez

Oct. 1, 2023

Congresswoman Alexandria Ocasio Cortez of New York gave a press conference in Manhattan at The Roosevelt Hotel on September 15th, 2023. The hotel has housed many migrants while the city figures out next steps. Since then, it was announced that migrants would be permitted to work and have a chance to become self sufficient. The hotel isn't the only place housing migrants and they have been seeking refuge from countries all around the world.

Parts of the world where migrants are coming to New York from:

South America

A large portion of refugees are escaping conditions in countries in South America. A majority come from Venezuela, but refugees also hail from Ecuador, Peru and Colombia among other countries.

Africa

Migrants and refugees are also coming from several countries in Africa like Burundi and Chad. Especially from West African countries like Senegal and Mauritania.

Europe

Migrants are also escaping war-torn countries like Ukraine. New York City has accepted thousands of Ukrainian refugees.

North America

Mexico is also seeing many refugees flee the country into New York, with most South American refugees making their way through the country to reach the U.S. south border.
